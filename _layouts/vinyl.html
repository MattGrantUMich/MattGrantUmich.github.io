My Vinyl Collection

When my dad was at Syracuse University, he ran a record shop which was pretty successful. In those efforts, he left campus with quite a large collection of records. Because of this, I have been lucky enough to get my hands on some truly great records, which I am very thankful for. My goal is to always improve this collection, and of course listen to and appreciate music the way it was meant to be listened to.

I will be updating this page from time to time, so feel free to swing by from time to time!
